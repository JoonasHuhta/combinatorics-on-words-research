The word aa2f23379 displayed in condensed form

This word over three letters {a,b,c} is abelian square-free (aa2f) except for the shortest allowed squares of period 1, i.e., only the case |u| = |v| = 1 is allowed for its factors uv, where u and v are commutatively equivalent words. The word and its 2000 character longer version of length 25579 was found with the aid of a parallelized Rust program written by Alexandr Gavrilenko in 2017.

The reading direction for each line is from bottom to top and from left to right. The 7 main lines are read from top to bottom.
