Structures in g85

When coloured by every second letter, factors look coloured with one colour as follows: 

g85(a) =  abcacdcbcdcadcdbdaba
cabadbabcbdbcbacbcdcacbabdabacadcbcdcac
dbcbacbcdcacdcbdcdadbdcbca

The image words of the abelian square-free g ≡ g85 endomorphism are obtained by cyclic permutation σ : a → b, b → c, c → d, d → a. So g(b) = σ(g(a)), g(c) = σ(g(b)), g(d) = σ(g(c)). The image word g(a) was found after an intensive computer-assisted search and is the shortest possible of its kind.

Below are some of the special factors that appear in image words. The reason for their appearance is not known.

A cyclic permutation of a factor of length 26, and the original factor, reappear in every image word in a surprising way: 

g85(a) = abcacdcbcdcadcdbdaba 
cabadbabcbdbcbacbcdcacbabd abacadcbcdcac
dbcbacbcdcacdcbdcdadbdcbca

g85(b) = bcdbdadcdadbadacabcb 
dbcbacbcdcacdcbdcdadbdcbca bcbdbadcdadbd
acdcbdcdadbdadcadabacadcdb

g85(c) = cdacabadabacbabdbcdc 
acdcbdcdadbdadcadabacadcdb cdcacbadabaca
bdadcadabacabadbabcbdbadac

g85(d) = dabdbcbabcbdcbcacdad 
bdadcadabacabadbabcbdbadac dadbdcbabcbdb 
cabadbabcbdbcbacbcdcacbabd

A factor of length 12 appears the same more than once: 

g85(a) = abcacdcbcdcadcdbdaba 
cabadbabcbdbcbacbcdcacbabd abacadcbcdcac
dbcbacbcdcacdcbdcdadbdcbca

This can also be thought of as the first factor w = cabadbabcbdb reappearing as σ2(w) = acdcbdcdadbd, where the cyclic permutation is applied twice.
Note that σ2 : a ↔ c, b ↔ d is its own inverse operation.
Both factors w and σ2(w) are highlighted below: 

g85(a) = abcacdcbcdcadcdbdaba 
cabadbabcbdbcbacbcdcacbabd abacadcbcdcac
dbcbacbcdcacdcbdcdadbdcbca

In fact, all the permutations of w of the form σi(w); i = 0,1,2; appear in g85(a), and σ1(w) appears twice.

Note also that w appears exactly as it is not only in g85(a), but also in g85(c) and twice in g85(d); see the weak emphasis on w in the image words above. Naturally, all cyclic permutations σi(w); i = 0,1,2,3; have corresponding appearances.

Some additional structures

Far more than half of the iterated string is grey, consisting of factors in which letters  a, b, c, d  occur equally often: 

g85(a) = abc acdcbcdcadcdbdabacabadbabcbd 
bcbacbc dcacbabd abac adcb cdc acdb
cbacbcdcacdcbdcd adbdcbca
 
g85(a) = abc acdcbcdcadcdbdabacabadbabcbd 
bcbacbcdcacba bdabacadcbcd c acdb
cbacbcdcacdcbdcd adbdcbca

All a-2-free factors of length 4, except  
{acbd, adbc, bacd, bdca, cadb, cbda, dbac, dcab}
occur in the iterated string.
