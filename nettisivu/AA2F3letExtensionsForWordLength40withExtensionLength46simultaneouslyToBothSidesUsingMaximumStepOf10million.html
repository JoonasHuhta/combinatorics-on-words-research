Aa2f words of length 40 over {a,b,c} and their aa2f extensions with extension length 46 simultaneously to both sides and using a maximum step of 10 million
( aa2f: abelian square-free except for the shortest allowed squares of period 1 )

The intensive computation was performed from Rovaniemi, Finland, in several phases in January-February 2026 on the THKMesh|B200 CUDA computing environment generously provided by Ben Koo, Henry Koo and their PKC engineering team, as part of the Balinese GovTech Subproject for Data Sovereignty.

The aa2f extensions divided the original aa2f words of length 40 into three categories: Extendable (so far), Undecided (so far) and NonExtendable (clear situation).

Over {a,b,c}, there are a total of 4688961678 aa2f words of length 40. For the computation, redundant permutations and their mirror images were removed from these words. After the extensions mentioned in the title, 124318432 words that look good so far remained.
Words that look ‘good’ here naturally mean the union of the above-mentioned categories, or sets Extendable (so far) and Undecided (so far).

The remaining words are less than 32% of the original words. Here, the extra permutations and their mirror images have been taken into account.
Thus, in the 3-letter case, more than 68% of the aa2f words of length 40 cannot be internal factors of a long aa2f word; in particular, they cannot be factors in image words when using morphisms to produce a desired pattern-free word.
